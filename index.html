Welcome To My Portfolio

Hello, my name is Can Aydin. I am a 32-year-old individual with a rich cultural background, being half Turkish and half American. My journey began on November 13, 1993, in Kadikoy, Istanbul, Turkey, where I spent the early years of my life. I started my education in Istanbul and continued there until the 4th grade. At that point, my family relocated to Dubai, UAE, due to my father’s work. Adjusting to a new environment at a young age was challenging, especially as I had just begun to feel at home in Istanbul. However, I embraced the change and continued my education in Dubai from the 5th grade onward. Dubai, with its vibrant and multicultural atmosphere, offered me invaluable life experiences. Living in one of the most international cities in the world, I formed friendships with people from diverse cultures and backgrounds. These interactions broadened my perspective, helping me understand and appreciate different ways of life. Over the years, I’ve encountered people from various walks of life and observed how different experiences shape individuals. During my time in Dubai, I faced a profound personal loss with the passing of my mother. This was a life-altering event that deeply impacted me, teaching me resilience and maturity. While it’s something I’ll never fully overcome, I’ve learned to live with it and draw strength from those memories. Academically, I pursued my passion for technology and creativity by studying Games Programming at university. Completing this course marked a significant milestone in my personal and professional journey. After finishing my studies, I decided to return to my roots and moved back to Istanbul, Turkey. My life has been a blend of diverse experiences and cultures, shaping me into someone who values adaptability, resilience, and global perspectives. These qualities, combined with my technical expertise in Games Programming, define who I am and what I bring to the table.
